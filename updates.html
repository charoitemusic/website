"I'm My Favorite Person" Release Day! 3/18/2022

Today's the day!

*What's the story behind this song?* "I'm My Favorite Person" is really about learning to love yourself. Every time we face rejection, this can get a little harder - that's why it's important to anchor yourself. Know the qualities you appreciate in others and see those qualities in yourself. I wrote about my own qualities - reliability and thoughtfulness- and how I am the kind of the friend I want to be to others, and the kind of friend I'd like to meet. üòÅ

This song also draws a bit from my experience as a nonamorous person (although you don't need to be to relate to it). Nonamory means "not looking for a partner". I'm not someone who falls in love, or lust. I'm not seeking out a significant other, or a "favorite person". I am it. I am the one that will always be there, so I'd better learn to enjoy my own company and my own self!

It's a celebration song. So celebrate yourself, and sing along! Feel free to change the words to fit whatever YOU imagine. In the coming days, I'll release an instrumental version so you can make your own if you'd like! Enjoy!
