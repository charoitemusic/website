Charoite is a multigenre singer-songwriter and violinist based in Indianapolis.

Charoite's name is inspired by the gemstone, charoite, which is believed to bestow clarity of purpose and the freedom to change. Music has a similar effect; often guiding and comforting us through pivotal moments in life. Charoite provides a voice of encouragement, humor, and acceptance.

Incorporating LED lights, costume design and various crafts into their performances and music videos, Chariote's style blends art deco, visual kei, and electronic fashion.
