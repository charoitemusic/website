About | Discography | Updates

Named after a purple gemstone believed to guide leaps of faith to success, Charoite encourages listeners to explore, experiment, and defend their joy with confidence. Charoite's music bounces from pop-rock to alt-rock and pop-punk, and matches it with visual kei flair and glowing accents. Charoite is also a programmer and well-rounded technologist, and intends to raise the bar for interactivity in virtual concerts with their own performances.

"Hopepunk is about demanding a better, kinder world, and truly believing that we can get there." - Alexandra Rowland

Charoite is no stranger to hardship. Born into an abusive family, exiled into poverty, and hammered by a series of uncommon and unfortunate events, Charoite only survived through the kindness and support of her local community. Having seen both the best and the worst in humanity, they took a chance on the world and turbo-charged their efforts to survive. After working five part-time jobs while attending school full time, Charoite finally graduated into a more stable life. Now they are an advocate for children's rights, promote access to the arts, and provide anti-bias training for technologists.
